# Product Vision: FarmLink Africa

## 1. Executive Summary
FarmLink Africa is a decentralized platform connecting Farmers, Buyers, and Transporters. Our goal is to eliminate middlemen, provide transparent pricing, and build a robust supply chain powered by modern AI and payment systems.

## 2. Target Audience
- **Farmers**: Needing access to larger markets and fair pricing.
- **Buyers**: Looking for reliable, trackable, and bulk agricultural produce.
- **Transporters**: Providing logistics services connecting farm gate to warehouse.

## 3. Core Capabilities
- Direct produce marketplace
- Integrated logistics booking
- Real-time market analytics
- Secure escrow payments
