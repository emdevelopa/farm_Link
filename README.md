# FarmLink Protocol

### Open Agricultural Commerce Infrastructure Built on Stellar

FarmLink Protocol is an open-source platform that connects farmers, buyers, and logistics providers through a transparent agricultural marketplace powered by the Stellar network.

Our mission is to reduce friction in agricultural trade by providing trusted payments, transparent transactions, logistics coordination, and produce traceability for emerging economies.

---

## Problem

Agricultural supply chains remain highly fragmented.

Farmers often struggle to:

* Reach verified buyers
* Receive timely payments
* Access broader markets
* Coordinate transportation

Buyers face challenges including:

* Limited visibility into produce availability
* Unverified suppliers
* Lack of transparency
* Payment risks

Transporters often operate without access to a centralized marketplace for delivery opportunities.

These inefficiencies increase costs, create trust issues, and limit economic growth across agricultural communities.

---

## Solution

FarmLink Protocol provides a digital infrastructure layer that enables:

* Farmer-to-buyer marketplaces
* Produce discovery
* Logistics coordination
* Escrow-based payments
* Agricultural supply chain tracking
* Digital reputation systems
* Future decentralized agricultural finance services

---

## Why Stellar

FarmLink is built on the Stellar ecosystem to leverage:

* Fast transactions
* Low transaction fees
* Stablecoin support
* Global accessibility
* Smart contract capabilities through Soroban

Stellar enables secure and transparent financial interactions between agricultural stakeholders without requiring expensive intermediaries.

* Delivery management

* Route coordination

### Escrow Payments

Powered by Stellar and Soroban.

Workflow:

Buyer Funds Escrow

↓

Produce Delivered

↓

Buyer Confirms Delivery

↓

Funds Released To Farmer
